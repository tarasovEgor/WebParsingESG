Folder used for storing the found PDF files across the webpages.

Each company will get its own folder under the corresponding name that follows this pattern:

--/downloaded_data/
    --/{company_1}_{INN}/
        -- PDF_file_1
        -- PDF_file_2
        ...
        -- PDF_file_n
    --/{company_2}_{INN}/
        ...
    --/{company_n}_{INN}/
